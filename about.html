Sadeem Sajid.

Is it not right to live and die as a man mastering his mental faculty? A question that prompts me to know life in each breath, in each cup of tea, and in each word that I intercept. A major reason to get this project see the dawn of reality was to understand what it means to live a life of purpose, in hope one day a wayfarer would then see the genius of Islam, and how the courses of its honor go deep within human society.

From game development, to graphic design, the realm of computers remain yet to be explored. However, I do feel my calling lies elsewhere, in a higher purpose. Regardless, I devote myself to this duty, however, this should not be the case all my life, this meaningless slaughter of the self is unnacceptable. Right?
